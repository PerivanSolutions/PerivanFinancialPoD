Our top priority when giving an estimate is to avoid future disagreement over the invoice. We therefore quote complete project costs, including unlimited corrections over the specified proof stages and no extra charges for fast turnarounds.

This can make our estimates appear more expensive than others but our final invoices are usually lower. Why do we do it?

  • Because we want to build long term relationships with our clients and invoice disagreements spoil relationships and undermine trust
  • Because it saves us money – we don’t need to employ an army of costing clerks
  • Because we like the culture throughout our company to be open and transparent

So when comparing estimates from financial printers please read the small print. Perivan’s won’t take long.
